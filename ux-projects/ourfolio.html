Design

Ourfolio is a mobile platform that aims to foster exclusively internet-based relationships, where artists and creatives communicate and collaborate online. It allows project exposure while helping them improve their current portfolio, empowering artistic ability, community, and network.

Discovery

problems
Several creatives go through several issues in relation to social media: • Users seeing AI-generated work through online spaces • Creatives struggling to finish personal projects • Creatives lacking genuine engagement, leading to little to no feedback on their work
audience
Through our research, our audience consists of all kinds of creatives, 18 years and up, including the following disciplines: • Graphic Designers • Animators / Motion Designers • Musicians • Illustrators
questions

• How will this app create relationships between its users?

• What features do users need as creatives to make feedback and engagement more effective?

• What will motivate users to create and finish projects while engaging with social media?

Design

user interface
Ourfolio consists of a complementary color combination, which symbolizes our mission for the artists to help each other grow. Orange and blue hues represent motivation, which reflects Ourfolio's mission to motivate and unite the creative community to make their dream career a reality.
key features

• Prompts: A page that encourages users to work on small challenges as personal projects to add to their portfolio.

• Connections: Each user can request to connect to other users to build their network of creatives.

• Uploading Posts: Users can post their work either as pieces or as a contribution to a new prompt to inspire a community to create.

Testing

usability

After testing the app, we were able to highlight:

• Users appreciating its clean layout and intuitive navigation.

• Users having quick access to their flight and travel information.

• A virtual assistant communicating with passengers on their needs.
