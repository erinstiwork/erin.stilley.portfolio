Under a mentorship from Grant Lau, the creative director for Amazon Prime's Thursday Night Football, I designed a title redesign for 2025.
‍

CONCEPT: PRIDE OF ALL TEAMS
People in their home cities are proud of where they are from and they strive to be represented. We dive into explorations of text and onomatopoeia to emphasize the excitement and intensity of football and its team’s colors.

WARPED TEXT AND ONOMATOPOEIA
Words shown through the screen create vivid imagery and evoking an auditory response for the viewers.

CONCEPT: SHOWCASING LOCATION
For every week, the city where the game takes place  will be represented as their football team’s colors. Monuments will be displayed to make them feel right at home.
